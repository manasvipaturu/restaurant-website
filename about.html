Family-Owned Indian Restaurant in Atlanta

Swagruhas Kitchen Deccan Flavors is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving authentic Indian food in Atlanta. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring that every dish we serve is filled with love and tradition.

We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients and traditional spices to bring the rich flavors of India to your plate.
